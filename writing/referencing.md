## 1.1 Format

### 1.1.1 Books

Reference entire book:

```text
[Author surname], initial. (Year) *Book title*. City: Publisher
```

### 1.1.2 Websites

General Web Pages:

```text
[Author surname], [initial.] [(Year)] [*Page title*]. Available at: [URL] (Accessed: [day] [month] [year])
```
